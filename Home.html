ABOUT US...

Silent Summit Resort offers breathtaking alpine skiing and excellent cabin lodging all in one unique location. Located in the mountians near the base of Le Sommet Muet, the weather here is consistant 32 degrees and there is always snow! As for skiing, there is almost 10,000 skiable acres here for you to explore. When coming off the slopes, make sure to stop in the brand-new Summit Lodge for refreshments. We think we really do have something for everyone, please feel free to check out our attractions!
